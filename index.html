Initiative titled AIOI cinema is an invitation to participate in film screening rituals, using room A101, open to all of EKA community.

Kickstarted by Aurelijus and animation department, the goal is to establish weekly tradition and foster engagement and ideas from all of EKA students. Please leave screening suggestions in the google doc. The optimal timing of screenings and choosing of films is still being worked out through experimentation. In some cases, we may arrange renting or access to some hard-to-get films with help of EKA

This webpage will be maintained and updated as details change. Write to aurelijus.ciupas@artun.ee if you want to chat about this initiative.
