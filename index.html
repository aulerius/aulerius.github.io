"AIOI cinema" is an invitation to partake in regular rituals of experiencing film in our very own cinema room, the A101, or AIOI "ayaoi", or AlOl "alol".

This initiative is kickstarted by Aurelijus and animation department, with a goal of making use of school screening facilities and enabling engagement from all of EKA community. Please leave screening suggestions in the google doc. The optimal timing of screenings and choosing of films is still being worked out through experimentation. In some cases, we could arrange renting or access to some hard-to-get films with the help of school.

This webpage will be maintained and updated as details change. Write to aurelijus.ciupas@artun.ee if you want to chat about this initiative.
